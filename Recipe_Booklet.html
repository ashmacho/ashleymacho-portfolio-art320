Recipe Booklet

March 2024

Layout design is a type of design that is often overlooked. I had no idea what I was talking into when taking this class. But I soon learned about the importance of fonts, heiarchy, and balancing a page. So when tasked to create a recipe book, I decided to make it about low-calorie local food. I named by book "Lo-Cal." The I searched for my favorite recipes. I decided to go for an organic and minimalistic design. I drew my own illustrations and designed the entire booklekt. Who knew you could be healthy and still eat good!
